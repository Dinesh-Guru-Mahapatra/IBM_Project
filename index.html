Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a hospital appointment?

Booking an appointment is simple! Search for hospitals in your city, select your preferred hospital, choose a date and time slot, and confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation with all details.

Is DocFinder free to use?

Yes, DocFinder is completely free for patients. We don't charge any fees for searching hospitals or booking appointments. Our service is funded through partnerships with healthcare providers.

How are hospitals verified?

We have a rigorous verification process that includes checking medical licenses, facility accreditation, doctor qualifications, and patient feedback. We regularly update our verification to ensure quality.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours in advance through your booking confirmation. For emergencies, our support team can assist with last-minute changes.

Do you have hospitals in rural areas?

We're continuously expanding our network to include hospitals in rural and semi-urban areas. Currently, we cover major cities and are working to include more regional healthcare centers.
